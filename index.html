Telegram Mini App + Web

Crypto Marketing Flow Builder

A single front-end experience that captures project basics, builds a recommended marketing funnel, shortlist influencers, assembles a budgeted plan, and submits a lead to your agency — all in under 4 minutes.

Product goal

Collect core project inputs and turn them into a customizable funnel, influencer shortlist, and marketing plan with budgets and timelines.

User outcome

  • Editable funnel flow with drag & drop stages.
  • Influencer & media shortlist (v1 placeholders).
  • Plan with services, KPIs, pricing, and agency fee.

Lead capture

Submit contact details to receive the plan by email and notify the agency via Telegram + email.

Guided flow (8 steps, no skips)

1

Project type

Single-select cards (DeFi, Memecoin, GameFi, NFT, SocialFi, RWA, Infrastructure, AI x Web3, Gambling, Other).

Mascot says: “Pick the closest match so the funnel feels tailored.”
2

Project stage

Horizontal roadmap slider from Concept → Post TGE with snap-to-step behavior.

Mascot says: “Stage shapes the urgency and channels we unlock.”
3

Dates & prep window

Launch date picker + prep-days slider (0-30). Kickoff auto-calculated as launch minus prep days.

Mascot says: “More prep days unlock deeper media and KOL options.”
4

Budget

USD slider (1k–300k) with tiered agency fee and execution budget breakdown.

Mascot says: “Every budget level reveals a wider toolkit.”
5

Funnel builder

Drag-and-drop sequence with mandatory and optional blocks, dependency warnings, and block library.

Mascot says: “Reorder freely — we’ll flag risky dependencies.”
6

Influencer & media shortlist

Recommended shortlist with placeholder profiles, filters, and selection counter.

Mascot says: “Fast activation options show up when prep time is tight.”
7

Plan builder

Editable service plan with KPIs, timelines, quantities, and auto-scale vs manual adjustments.

Mascot says: “Tell us what you cover in-house and we’ll remove it.”
8

Lead form & submit

Capture email, Telegram handle, website, optional links, consent, and send full snapshot to agency.

Mascot says: “Share your contacts to get the full plan delivered.”

Core entities (v1)

Entity Purpose Key fields
Session Progress tracking + platform context session_id, created_at, platform, telegram_user_id, locale
Answers User input capture project_type_id, stage_id, kickoff_date, launch_date, prep_days, budget_total_usd
Funnel Ordered block flow with dependencies blocks[{block_id, position, is_mandatory, params}]
Influencers Placeholder shortlist (v1) selected[], category_filters
Plan Service items with pricing + timeline items[{service_id, duration_days, quantity, unit_price_usd}]
Lead Submission payload + contacts email, telegram_handle, website_url, consent, full_payload_snapshot

Recommendation engine (rule-based)

Inputs

  • Project type, stage, prep days, execution budget.
  • Influencer category emphasis based on project fit.

Outputs

  • Funnel template ID + ordered blocks.
  • Recommended services with priorities.
  • Influencer category mix for shortlist.

Rule storage

All rules live in backend config for quick iteration without frontend deployments.

Non-functional focus

Performance

Mobile-first, 2-3s time-to-interactive target, session recovery on reload.

Security + spam

Telegram initData validation, web rate limits, optional captcha for browser sessions.

Analytics

Step tracking, drop-off metrics, budget change events, and lead conversion KPIs.

Acceptance checks (v1)

No skipped steps, funnel + plan editing, fee calculations, influencer selection, and lead submission with full snapshot logging.

UI language stays English for user-facing screens. Admin and system messaging can remain English as well.
